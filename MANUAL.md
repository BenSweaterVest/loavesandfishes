## THE STORY

Once upon a time, in ancient Galilee, a carpenter's son from Nazareth decided to start a ministry. Pretty standard stuff. Except in this timeline, the fish of the Sea of Galilee are *weaponized*, Jesus is basically a Pok√©mon trainer, and you're about to embark on the weirdest JRPG of your life.

You are **Jesus of Nazareth**, fresh out of the carpentry business and ready to change the world. Your mission: spread your message, recruit twelve extremely questionable disciples, and battle your way through ancient Israel using trained fish as weapons. Because apparently, that's a thing now.

The fish of Galilee aren't just fish‚Äîthey're **weapon fish**, blessed (or cursed, depending on who you ask) with elemental powers. As you travel from town to town, you'll catch these aquatic warriors, train them up, and use them to fight everything from skeptical scorpions to the literal embodiment of the seven deadly sins.

It's a story of faith, friendship, and *holy mackerels*.

Look, we didn't say it made sense. We just said it would be fun.

## YOUR JOURNEY

You are **Jesus of Nazareth**: carpenter-turned-teacher, miracle worker, and apparently the greatest fish trainer in history. Your dad's a carpenter, your mom's Mary, and you're about to have the weirdest career change ever.

### YOUR ABILITIES

**As the Teacher:**
- Recruit disciples (twelve of them, and they're... a mixed bag)
- Perform miracles when your miracle meter fills (limit breaks, basically)
- Team up with fish for combo attacks (yes, really)
- Heal your fish party (they get hurt a lot)
- Turn the tide of battle with faith (and also fish)

**Your Stats:**
- **Faith**: How strong your miracles are (max it out)
- **Wisdom**: Strategy and knowing when to run (underrated)
- **Compassion**: Healing power (you'll need it)
- **Courage**: Resist fear attacks (there are a lot of scary fish)

### YOUR MISSION

1. **Spread the Word** - Visit all thirteen towns (tourism!)
2. **Gather the Twelve** - Recruit disciples (lower your expectations)
3. **Collect the Parables** - Find 25 teachings (some are in weird places)
4. **Defeat the Darkness** - Fight bosses in each town (with fish)
5. **Reach Jerusalem** - Complete your ministry (bring tissues)
