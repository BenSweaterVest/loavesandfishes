## THE STORY

In ancient times, when the Roman Empire stretched across the known world, a carpenter's son from Nazareth embarked on a journey that would change history forever. But this is not merely a retelling of ancient scripture‚Äîthis is YOUR story, where faith meets adventure in ways never imagined.

You are **Jesus of Nazareth**, beginning your ministry in the Galilee region. Your mission: to spread the word, perform miracles, and gather twelve disciples who will help you on your journey. But in this world, the journey takes an unexpected turn...

The fish of the Sea of Galilee have been blessed with mysterious powers. These aren't ordinary fish‚Äîthey're **weapon fish**, creatures imbued with elemental energies that reflect the spiritual battle between light and darkness. As you travel through ancient Israel, you'll collect these sacred fish, train them in battle, and use them to overcome the forces of evil that seek to stop your ministry.

It's a story of faith, friendship, and holy mackerels.

## YOUR JOURNEY

You are **Jesus of Nazareth**, carpenter's son turned teacher and miracle worker. In this world, you walk a unique path‚Äîgathering not just followers, but sacred fish that fight alongside you in spiritual combat.

### YOUR ABILITIES

**As the Teacher:**
- Recruit disciples (the twelve apostles)
- Perform miracles when your miracle meter fills
- Combine forces with your fish in devastating combo attacks
- Heal and revive your fish party
- Turn the tide of any battle with faith

**Your Stats:**
- **Faith**: Determines miracle power
- **Wisdom**: Affects strategy and combo availability
- **Compassion**: Healing effectiveness
- **Courage**: Resist fear and dark attacks

### YOUR MISSION

1. **Spread the Word** - Visit all thirteen towns
2. **Gather the Twelve** - Recruit all apostles to your cause
3. **Collect the Parables** - Find and understand 25 sacred teachings
4. **Defeat the Darkness** - Overcome enemies and bosses in each town
5. **Reach Jerusalem** - Complete your ministry

## BATTLE MECHANICS

### TURN ORDER

Battles flow in turns based on **Speed stats**:
1. Fastest fish/enemy acts first
2. Actions resolve in order
3. Turn ends, next turn begins

### YOUR TURN OPTIONS

**FIGHT**
- Choose a move
- Effectiveness calculated by type
- Critical hits possible (6.25% base chance)
- STAB bonus if move type matches fish type (+50%)

**ITEM**
- Use bread or equipment
- Item takes effect immediately
- Turn ends

**SWITCH**
- Swap active fish
- Free action (doesn't end turn)
- Use strategically to counter types

**APOSTLE ABILITY**
- Once per battle per apostle
- Powerful effects
- 3-turn cooldown

**MIRACLE**
- Costs miracle meter percentage
- Can turn the tide instantly
- Choose wisely

**COMBO**
- Fish + Apostle combination attack
- Requires 25% miracle meter
- Devastating damage

**RUN**
- Escape from wild battles (50% chance)
- Cannot run from trainer/boss battles
